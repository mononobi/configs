1. in `vpn` file, replace `{SERVER_ADDRESS}`, `{YOUR_PASSWORD}` and `{YOUR_USERNAME}` with
   correct ones.

2. if you do not have cisco installed, follow the instructions in `connection` file.

3. you could copy `vpn` file into `/usr/local/sbin/` and make it executable by `chmod 777 vpn`
   then you could run it by typing `vpn` in terminal.
